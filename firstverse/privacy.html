Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Data Collection

FirstVerse does NOT collect any personal information. We have no servers, no database, and no backend services processing your data. Your information cannot be collected because we have nowhere to store it.

Local Storage Only

All your data (bookmarks, preferences, reading history, semantic search activity) is stored only on your device in private app storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or to third parties.

Network Activity

FirstVerse is designed to run fully offline. All fonts, styling, Bible texts, semantic search models, and verse embeddings are bundled with the app and loaded locally from your device.

The app does not contact external servers for analytics, fonts, AI models, or search processing. Any network activity that occurs is the result of actions you choose, such as:

  • Opening the Privacy Policy or Terms of Use in your web browser
  • Tapping an email link to contact us
  • Following a Scripture reference or share action that opens another app or website

No Tracking

FirstVerse does not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting SDKs, or any tracking services. Your interaction with the app is completely private.

Third-Party Components

FirstVerse uses open source software components that run entirely on your device:

  • Transformers.js — JavaScript library for running AI models locally
  • ONNX Runtime — High-performance engine for AI model execution
  • MiniLM Embedding Model — Compact AI model enabling semantic search
  • Capacitor — Native runtime for Android functionality
  • Tailwind CSS — Styling framework

Important: All AI processing occurs locally on your device. No search queries, verse data, or study activity is sent to external servers. These components do not collect or transmit any data.

Third-Party Fonts

This app includes the following fonts, bundled locally for offline use:

  • Inter — Interface text (SIL Open Font License 1.1)
  • Merriweather — Scripture text (SIL Open Font License 1.1)

Open Source Licenses

A complete list of open source components and their licenses is available within the app under Settings → About → Open Source.

Your Control

You are in full control of your data. You can export your data (where available) from within the app, or delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage from your device settings. We cannot recover your data because we never receive or store it.

Summary: Zero data collection. Everything stays on your device. Core functionality works fully offline, including semantic search. Third-party components run locally and do not transmit data. You control your data at all times.
