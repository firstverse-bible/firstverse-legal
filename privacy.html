Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Data Collection

FirstVerse does NOT collect any personal information. We have no servers, no database, no backend. Your data cannot be collected because we have nowhere to store it.

Local Storage Only

All your data (bookmarks, preferences, reading history) is stored only on your device in private app storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Network Activity

FirstVerse loads resources from trusted CDNs on first use:

  • Fonts: Inter and Merriweather typefaces from Google Fonts (one-time download, cached)
  • Styling: Tailwind CSS framework from jsDelivr CDN (one-time download, cached)
  • Semantic Search: AI models (~25MB) from jsDelivr/HuggingFace CDNs (one-time download, cached)

After initial downloads, everything is cached and the app works completely offline. Your search queries are NOT sent anywhere—they run entirely on your device.

No Tracking

FirstVerse does not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any tracking services. Your interaction is completely private.

Your Control

Export your data anytime via Settings → Export Data. Delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage. We cannot recover your data because we never had access to it.

Summary: Zero data collection. Everything stays on your device. One-time model download for search. Fully offline operation. You're in complete control.
